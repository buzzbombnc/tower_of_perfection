About this website

This website is a place for me to write about technology, show off various projects and keep my writing skills sharp. Perhaps I'll complain about politics, add recipes, or talk personal finance as well! We shall see.

My longer term plans are to continue updating the site in general, including the software, design and anything else that strikes my fancy.

As for the name, it is a smart ass reply to the oh-so-corporate term "center of excellence". If you've ever been stuck in Corporate America and heard that term, you know exactly why the term is a joke!
